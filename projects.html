My First Application - Virtual Bookshelf
Solution Neutral Problem Statement
Carrying heavy books in public places, and lack of storage space to store them
Goal
Creating a digital solution to store and access books with ease
Research
Primary and Secondary Research
In the secondary research I tried to understand the different ways in which people read; it was either through books and printouts (physical medium) or through e-books and articles on social media (digital medium). On further research, I found that the most common way to store books where desks and shelves. On the other hand, in digital media, applications with e-books were created to access multiple books. However, if the user had a pre-existing book or article saved elsewhere, they would have to navigate back to that particular application. After conducting interviews, I concluded that:
1. Users need an easy way to access their books
2.Users do not like to carry books around, but would love to read while travelling
3.Books are being used at least 2-4 times in a day
4.Books require space and maintanence
Persona
Requirement List
1. Easy access of books at any time of the day
2. Reducing weight of the books
3. Making carrying the books around easier
4. Returning/renewing the books
5. Access of books on a larger platform
6. Aesthetically pleasing environment
7. Making notes on printed copies of the book
